Game Idea

Match Valley combines classic match-3 gameplay with light renovation and progression mechanics.
By completing puzzles, players earn rewards that allow them to restore buildings, unlock new areas, and bring life back to the valley. Look, most match-3 games? You play 'em for like a week and forget about them. But this one actually makes you want to check back in to see how your valley's shaping up.

Match Gameplay

Boosters

To keep puzzles interesting, the game features:

  • Tons of different boosters that all do their own thing

  • Combinations that reward smart planning

  • The game gets harder bit by bit (which is way better than sudden difficulty spikes)

  • When you finally crack a tough level, it feels earned - not like the game was just being mean to you.
