Boosters

To keep puzzles interesting, the game features:

  • Tons of different boosters that all do their own thing

  • Tricky spots where you actually have to think, not just hope for the best

  • Combinations that reward smart planning

  • The game gets harder bit by bit (which is way better than sudden difficulty spikes)

  • When you finally crack a tough level, it feels earned - not like the game was just being mean to you.
